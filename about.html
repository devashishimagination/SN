Our Story

2024

The Beginning

Saturnx Nexus was founded by a group of tech industry veterans who recognized the growing gap between traditional education and industry needs. With an initial investment of $2 million, we launched our first three courses: Web Development, Data Science, and Cybersecurity.

Rapid Growth

Within our first year, we expanded our course offerings to include Mobile App Development, Cloud Computing, and Artificial Intelligence. Our student body grew from 100 to over 1,000, and we established partnerships with 50+ tech companies for internships and job placements.
