Placement FAQs

Common questions about our placement process and career support.

Are internships also facilitated through the Placement Cell?

Yes. The Placement Cell actively collaborates with companies to provide internship opportunities, particularly for pre-final year students.

Who is eligible to participate in the placement process?

Most of our graduates find employment within 3-6 months of completing their course. However, this can vary based on factors such as your location, the job market, your prior experience, and how actively you participate in the job search process.

What kind of companies hire your graduates?

All final-year students who meet the eligibility criteria set by the Placement Cell and the recruiting organizations are eligible. This typically includes academic performance, attendance, and conduct..

What is the typical placement process followed by companies?

Most companies follow a structured recruitment process, which may include:
Online Aptitude/Technical Test
Group Discussion (optional)
Technical Interview(s)
HR Interview

How can students prepare for placements?

Students are encouraged to:
Attend placement training programs
Practice aptitude and coding problems
Build a strong resume
Prepare well for interviews by reviewing academic concepts and project work.
